Things to keep in mind

Before securing a rental car, it's crucial to possess a valid driver's license. Familiarize yourself with insurance options and be wary of hidden fees. Inspect the vehicle's condition, understand the fuel policy, and be aware of mileage limits. Acquaint yourself with local traffic regulations and grasp return policies. Plan ahead to ensure the rental car aligns with both your needs and budget. Following are things to keep in mind.....

  • Age Restrictions: Be aware of the rental company's age requirements.
  • Payment Policies: Understand the payment methods accepted and inquire about any pre-authorization holds on your credit card.
  • Reservation Details: Double-check your reservation details, including pick-up and drop-off locations, dates, and times.
  • Return Conditions: Know the return conditions, such as the time the car needs to be returned and where to drop off the keys if returning after hours.
